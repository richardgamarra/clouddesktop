## 1. Getting Started

CloudDesktop Mobile is a web app that works like a native app on your phone. You can access it from any mobile browser at:

**https://clouddesktop.infoplay.com/mobile**

If you visit the site from a desktop browser, it will automatically show the desktop version. Mobile browsers are redirected to the mobile view automatically.

---

## 2. Installing the App (PWA)

Installing CloudDesktop as an app on your home screen gives you a full-screen experience with no browser address bar — just like a native app.

### Android (Chrome) — Recommended

1. Open Chrome and go to **https://clouddesktop.infoplay.com/mobile**
2. Tap the **⋮ three-dot menu** in the top-right corner of Chrome
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"**
4. A dialog appears with two options:
   - **"This app is already installed → Click to open the app instead"** ← tap this one if it appears (opens the full PWA)
   - **"Create shortcut – Shortcuts open in Chrome"** ← avoid this one (opens inside Chrome)
5. If installing for the first time, tap **"Add"** on the confirmation popup
6. The CloudDesktop icon will be added to your **App Drawer** (swipe up from home screen)

### Finding the Icon After Install

The icon may land in the App Drawer instead of your home screen:

1. Swipe **up** from the bottom of your home screen to open the App Drawer
2. Look for **"CloudDesktop"** with the cloud icon
3. Press and **hold** the icon, then drag it to your home screen

> **Tip:** You can also search for "CloudDesktop" in your phone's app search to find it quickly.

### iOS (Safari)

1. Open Safari and go to **https://clouddesktop.infoplay.com/mobile**
2. Tap the **Share button** (⬆ box with arrow) at the bottom of Safari
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **Add** — the icon will appear on your home screen

### Already Installed?

If you see the "This app is already installed" message in Chrome, that means the PWA is on your device. Tap that first option to open it, then find the icon in your App Drawer and drag it to your home screen.

## 3. Logging In

1. Open CloudDesktop (from home screen icon or browser)
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Tap **Sign In**

After logging in, you are taken directly to the mobile dashboard. The login page is removed from your navigation history so pressing back does not return to it.

---

## 4. Navigation Overview

The mobile interface has two fixed bars:

| Bar | Location | Purpose |
|-----|----------|---------|
| **Top Bar** | Top of screen | Shows app name + your account avatar |
| **Bottom Tab Bar** | Bottom of screen | Switch between Apps, Notes, Bookmarks, Settings |

Tap any tab at the bottom to switch views. Your current tab is highlighted.

---

## 5. Apps Tab

The Apps tab is your main launcher — it shows all your saved web apps organized into groups.

### Launching an App

- **Tap** any app icon to open it in a new browser tab

### App Groups

- Apps are organized by **colored groups** (e.g., Google, Work, Personal)
- Each group shows its name and a colored dot
- A **＋ button** appears in the top-right of each group header to add a new app to that group

### Adding an App

1. Tap the **＋** button in any group header
2. Fill in the app details (name, URL, icon)
3. Save

### Editing or Deleting an App

1. **Long-press** (hold for ~0.5 seconds) any app icon
2. A menu slides up from the bottom with options:
   - **✎ Edit App** — change name, URL, or icon
   - **🗑 Delete App** — remove the app
   - **✕ Cancel** — dismiss the menu

## 6. Notes Tab

The Notes tab opens **NotesVault** (notesvault.infoplay.com) embedded directly in CloudDesktop so you never leave the app.

### Using Notes

- The NotesVault interface loads inside the mobile view
- You can browse categories on the left panel, view and create notes
- Tap the **↗ open in browser** button (top-right corner) to open NotesVault in a full browser tab if needed

### If Notes Won't Load

If the embedded view shows a loading error or takes too long:
- Tap **"Open NotesVault ↗"** to open it directly in your browser
- This can happen on restricted networks

### Back Navigation Inside Notes

- Use the **← back arrow** inside NotesVault to go back within notes
- To return to the CloudDesktop tab bar, tap any tab at the bottom (Apps, Bookmarks, Settings)

---

## 7. Bookmarks Tab

The Bookmarks tab shows your saved bookmarks and news groups.

### Browsing

- Bookmarks are listed vertically, grouped by category
- Tap any bookmark to open it in a new browser tab

### News Groups

- News groups show the latest headlines from your configured sources
- Groups stack vertically on mobile for easy scrolling

---

## 8. Settings Tab

The Settings tab shows your account info and app preferences.

### Account Bar

At the top you'll see a single row with:
- Your **email address**
- Your **role** badge (e.g., admin, free)
- A **Sign out** button

### Theme

Toggle between **🌙 Dark** and **☀️ Light** mode. Your preference is saved and remembered next time you open the app.

### Switch to Desktop Version

Tap **"Switch to Desktop version →"** to open the full desktop dashboard. Useful when you need access to desktop-only features.

---

## 9. Top Bar & Account Menu

The top bar shows the CloudDesktop logo on the left and your **avatar** (a circle with your initial) on the right.

### Opening the Account Menu

Tap your **avatar** to open the dropdown menu. It shows:
- Your email address
- **📲 Install App** — shown only if the app is not yet installed as a PWA (see Section 2)
- **🚪 Sign out** — logs you out and returns to the login screen

### Install App Option

If you see "📲 Install App" in the menu:
- On **Android**: tapping it will trigger Chrome's native install prompt
- On **iOS** or if Chrome prompt isn't available: a step-by-step overlay appears explaining how to add the app to your home screen via the Share menu

Once the app is installed as a PWA (running standalone from the home screen icon), the Install App option disappears from the menu automatically.

---

## 10. Switching to Desktop View

You can switch to the full desktop version at any time:

**Option 1 — Settings tab:**
1. Tap **Settings** in the bottom tab bar
2. Tap **"Switch to Desktop version →"**

**Option 2 — Direct URL:**
- Navigate to **https://clouddesktop.infoplay.com/dashboard** in your browser

> **Note:** The desktop version is optimized for larger screens and has additional management features not available in the mobile view.
