## Desktop 1 — Getting Started

CloudDesktop is a personal cloud workspace that runs in your browser. Access it at:

**https://clouddesktop.infoplay.com**

Desktop browsers are directed to the full dashboard automatically. If you visit from a mobile device, you are redirected to the mobile view.

---

## Desktop 2 — Logging In

1. Go to **https://clouddesktop.infoplay.com**
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **Sign In**

You are taken directly to the dashboard. The login page is removed from your browser history so pressing Back does not return to it.

---

## Desktop 3 — Dashboard Overview

The desktop dashboard has two main areas:

| Area | Location | Purpose |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Sidebar** | Left side | Navigate between tabs (Apps, News, Bookmarks, Widgets, Notes) |
| **Main Content** | Right/center | Content for the selected tab |

A background image fills the screen behind the content. You can customize it in Settings.

---

## Desktop 4 — Apps Tab

The Apps tab is your main launcher — showing all saved web apps in colored groups.

### Launching an App
- **Click** any app icon to open it in a new browser tab

### App Groups
- Apps are organized by **colored groups** (e.g., Google, Work)
- Each group shows a colored dot and group name
- Click **+ Add Group** to create a new group

### Adding an App
1. Click **+ Add App** inside a group
2. Enter the app name and URL
3. Optionally set a custom icon (emoji or image URL)
4. Click Save

### Editing or Deleting an App
- **Right-click** any app icon to open the context menu
- Choose **Edit** to change name, URL, or icon
- Choose **Delete** to remove it

### Managing Groups
- Right-click a group header → **Edit Group** to rename or change color
- Right-click a group header → **Delete Group** to remove it and all its apps

---

## Desktop 5 — News & Bookmarks

### News Tab
- Displays headlines from your configured news sources
- Click any headline to open the article in a new tab
- News sources are organized into groups

### Bookmarks Tab
- Shows your saved bookmarks organized by category
- Click any bookmark to open it
- Right-click to edit or delete

## Desktop 7 — Notes Tab

The Notes tab opens **NotesVault** — your personal note-taking workspace.

- Create and organize notes into categories
- Use the left panel to browse categories
- Click any note to open and edit it
- Notes sync automatically when you save

---

## Desktop 8 — Settings & Backups

Access Settings from the sidebar or by navigating to **/settings**.

### Save to Cloud
Click **💾 Save to Cloud Now** to back up your entire workspace (apps, groups, news sources, tabs) to the server. You can restore it on any device.

### Restore from Cloud
Click **↩ Restore Latest Backup** to restore your most recent backup. Up to 5 backups are kept automatically (including daily auto-saves).

### Background Images
- Choose a preset background for **Dark Mode** or **Light Mode**
- Upload your own image (JPG, PNG, WebP — max 10MB)
- Paste any image URL
- Adjust the **overlay darkness** slider for better text readability

### Fix & Bake Icons
- **Fix Google & Microsoft Icons** — replaces generic icons with official Gmail, Drive, Outlook, Teams icons instantly
- **Bake All Icons Now** — fetches and permanently embeds all app icons as base64 images (works offline after baking)

### Local Export / Import
- **↓ Export JSON** — downloads all settings as a JSON file to your device
- **↑ Import JSON** — restores settings from a previously exported JSON file

### Recover Previous Workspace
If your workspace was previously saved with password encryption, click **🔑 Recover Now** and enter your CloudDesktop password to decrypt and restore it.

## Mobile 2 — Installing the App (PWA)

Install CloudDesktop as an app on your home screen for a full-screen experience with no browser bar.

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open Chrome → go to **https://clouddesktop.infoplay.com/mobile**
2. Tap the **⋮ three-dot menu** (top-right of Chrome)
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"**
4. If you see two options, tap **"This app is already installed → Click to open the app instead"** (full PWA, no Chrome)
5. Tap **"Add"** to confirm — the icon is added to your App Drawer

**Finding the icon after install:**
- Swipe up from the home screen to open the App Drawer
- Search for "CloudDesktop"
- Long-press the icon and drag it to your home screen

### iOS (Safari)

1. Open Safari → go to **https://clouddesktop.infoplay.com/mobile**
2. Tap the **Share button** (⬆) at the bottom
3. Scroll down → tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **Add** — the icon appears on your home screen

## Mobile 5 — Apps Tab

- Tap the **＋ button** in the top-right of any group header

## Mobile 6 — Notes Tab

Opens **NotesVault** embedded directly in CloudDesktop.

- Browse categories on the left panel
- Tap any note to open and edit it

- Tap any bottom tab to return to CloudDesktop

## Mobile 7 — Bookmarks Tab
